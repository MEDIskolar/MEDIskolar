About Us

Mission

The mission of this organization is to promote health literacy and service in the community and guide, engage, and develop the youth’s inclination to pursue medicine through accessible and comprehensive health educational and service campaigns aimed toward the masses.

The vision of this organization is an informed and well-served community equipped with health literacy and services, and consisting of young health advocates eager to serve the masses through their present and future activities.
